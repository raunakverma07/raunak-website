3rd Year: Clarity, Skills & Future Direction

(Sem 5 - Sem 6)

During my SSC preparation, I realized that General Science was becoming quite challenging, and I started thinking about exploring other career paths. That’s when I decided to shift my focus towards coding and technical skills, aiming for better career opportunities in the future.

I joined an institute to learn Data Science, where I completed Python along with important libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Streamlit. I also learned SQL, and currently, I am working on Machine Learning using Scikit-learn. In the upcoming phase, I will be learning Deep Learning and Generative AI as part of my course.

Alongside this, I also maintained better consistency in college. In the 5th semester, I attended more regularly and achieved an SGPA of 8.25. I am aiming to improve this further in the 6th semester.

At the beginning of 2026, I developed a strong interest in frontend development. Through YouTube, I learned HTML and CSS and started building website clones. Currently, I am working on my own portfolio website, applying everything I have learned so far.

My B.Sc degree will be completed in the next couple of months, and I am planning to pursue MCA as the next step in my academic journey.

Overall, this phase has given me clarity, direction, and a strong foundation in technical skills. It marks the transition from exploration to focused career building.
