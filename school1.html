Phase 1: Foundation Years

(Class 1 - 4)

I completed my early schooling at Crescent Public High School, where I built a strong academic foundation. During this phase, I consistently performed at the top of my class.

In Class 1, I secured 1st rank with 98%, followed by 2nd rank in Class 2 with 92%. I maintained my performance in Class 3 with 88% (2nd rank), and in Class 4, I achieved 82% with 3rd rank.

Apart from academics, this phase was filled with memorable friendships and joyful moments. I had a great group of friends, and on my last day at school, one of my closest friends surprised me with many thoughtful gifts, making it a truly emotional memory.

I was also one of the favorite students among teachers, known for my discipline and performance. When I decided to leave the school, even the principal encouraged me to stay and generously offered free education.

However, for better academic growth, I chose to move forward. This phase remains a crucial part of my journey, where I developed confidence, discipline, and a strong base for future learning.
