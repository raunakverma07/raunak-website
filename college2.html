2nd Year: Skill Building & Improvement

(Sem 3 - Sem 4)

In the third semester, due to SSC preparation, I was again unable to attend college regularly. However, one positive change during this time was that I switched my vocational subject from Physics to Cyber Security, which turned out to be very interesting.

Because of limited focus on college, my academic performance in the third semester was not very strong. However, I worked on improving myself and managed to cover up in the fourth semester, achieving an SGPA of 7.30.

During this period, a cricket tournament was organized in college where all matches were knockout-based. Our team also participated, and I was the captain of the team. Unfortunately, due to a lack of strong players, we lost our first match.

On a lighter note, my personal performance in that match was quite memorable — I faced just one last ball and ended up hitting a six on it.

In the fourth semester, I started attending college more regularly. As a result, I was able to build a small circle of 3–4 friends in the class, which made the college environment more comfortable and engaging.

With these small experiences, improvements, and memories, my second year came to an end, marking another step forward in my overall journey.
