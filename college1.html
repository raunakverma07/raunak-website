1st Year: Beginning of College Life

(New Journey & Adjustments)

After completing Class 12, I started preparing for NDA at CMC Defence Academy. At the same time, I took admission in Holkar Science College for my B.Sc degree. Initially, my major subject was Mathematics by default, which I later changed to Computer Science.

Due to NDA preparation, I was not able to attend college regularly. Because of this, I could not build many friendships in the beginning, and everything felt new and unfamiliar.

During this time, I developed a good friendship with one person who supported me a lot throughout my first year. She helped me with assignments, shared notes, and even explained mathematics concepts like a teacher. Her support played an important role in my academic journey during that phase.

In the second semester, I realized that due to my spectacles, NDA might not be a suitable path for me. After that, I joined a SSC batch in the same coaching institute.

During this time, I mostly focused on my coaching rather than college. I used to visit college only occasionally, mainly for examinations.

In the second semester, I also participated in a drawing competition where I made a sketch of Bhagat Singh and secured second prize along with a certificate.

I successfully passed both semesters, but due to limited focus on college, my CGPA remained around 6.4. Overall, this phase was about adjustment, self-realization, and balancing multiple priorities, and eventually I moved on to the second year of my college journey.
