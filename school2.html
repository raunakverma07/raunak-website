Phase 2: Sandipani Preparatory School

(Class 5 - 8)

I took admission in Sandipani Preparatory School in Class 5. This phase was one of the most enjoyable and balanced stages of my school life, where both academics and activities were given equal importance.

Teachers were supportive and provided enough time for sports and extracurricular activities. Along with studies, I was able to enjoy my school life fully.

During this phase, I made many friends. One of them is still with me till now. Our bond grew stronger over time, and we have continued our journey together from school to college like brothers.

In Class 6, I was selected as the Green House Wise Captain. Later, I served as Captain in Class 7 and 8, which helped me develop leadership qualities and responsibility.

Initially, the school was limited to Class 5, but gradually it expanded up to Class 8 for our batch. This allowed us to continue our journey in the same environment for a longer period.

This phase includes many memories such as traveling by auto, spending lunch breaks with friends, playing together, and going on picnics. These experiences made this phase truly special and unforgettable.
