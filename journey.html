My Journey

Throughout my school and college life, I have explored different paths while trying to understand where my true interest and strengths lie. Like many others, my early dream was to become a cricketer, driven by my passion for the sport.

During my higher secondary education, I chose PCM with a general thought of pursuing engineering. However, towards the end of school, I developed a strong interest in the defense sector and joined an NDA preparation academy with the aim of joining the Air Force.

As my journey progressed, my focus shifted again towards government services such as the Income Tax Department and CBI, which led me to prepare for SSC examinations. Each phase taught me something new and helped me understand my preferences better.

Eventually, during my college years, I discovered a clear and stable direction in the field of technology. I realized that this domain values knowledge, practical skills, and continuous learning over fixed criteria like cut-offs or theoretical scoring alone.

Today, I have chosen a path where growth depends on self-improvement, consistency, and real-world problem-solving ability. It is a field where I compete with myself, keep upgrading my skills, and move forward based on my capabilities.
